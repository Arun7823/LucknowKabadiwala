About Us

Lucknow Kabadiwala is a new start-up for recycling the waste. It provides a platform to sell your scrap online . You can save your time ,earn money and contribute to natural welfare. Scrap is picked up by verified pickup boy. The platform basically work as a door-step method.You can fill the contact form and our team will contact you as soon as possible or you can directly call us on given number.

Lucknow Kabadiwala is All-in-One solution for scrap sales at your door step. Our aim is to give you the hassle-free solution for scrap sales where you can contact us online by filling a simple form to schedule your pickup.


Rate cards are pre-defined which the seller can check online so means you're not needed to have any bargain at the time when we pick the scrap. We deal in every kind of the scrap so you can contact us even if the scrap category is not defined on the rate card.

We tried making our services very convenient where you can contact us by filling a simple online form using your mobile or PC to schedule the pickup and then our support team will contact you back to complete the further processes required.
